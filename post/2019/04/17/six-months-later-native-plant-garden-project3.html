Beautiful lupines blooming in my yard!

April 2019 lunine blooming

Native plants have been growing and growing in my yards. In this early spring, lots of lupine plants have been bloomed. As I reported in Jan 16, 2019, our yards have lupine plants from native plant seeds mix. Surprisingly lots of lupine plants grew bigger and had lots of beautiful violet flowers.
